Online Examination System

- By Satyavanth

Online Examination System is a web application that establishes a network between the institutes and the students. The system is designed for the large and small scale institutes that conduct the examination for the students. The system is designed to reduce the manual work and the paper work that is involved in the examination process.

Features of our Online Exam Application:

  • Secure and reliable platform for conducting exams
  • Flexible exam scheduling options
  • Support for various question types (multiple choice, true/false, essay, etc.)
  • Automatic grading and instant result generation
  • Real-time monitoring and proctoring capabilities
  • Customizable exam templates and question banks
  • Detailed performance analytics and reports
